Lounge-Lit House Concerts

Warmly vibed live music in a spacious home.

A few times a year, a music room in Santa Cruz opens to a small audience for evenings of real listening. Just beyond the glass, a lush garden waterfall sets the tone while instruments come alive and songs unfold in the same room as you.

Gracious host Eric Schneider welcomes us into his home to hear artists up close, linger in conversation, and let the night move at a human pace.
